QCapp v12 deployment checklist

1. Upload every file in this folder to the root of the proto-qc repository.
2. Replace index.html, sw.js, manifest.webmanifest, README.md, and the existing icon files.
3. Commit the changes and wait for GitHub Pages to finish deploying.
4. Open https://apdelany1.github.io/proto-qc/?v=12
5. On iPhone, completely close and reopen the installed home-screen app.
6. Start a new inspection and confirm Grading is off by default.
7. Add packaging once on Details, then confirm it is selectable on an inspection row.
8. Import a CSV or .xlsx manifest, filter to one container, select multiple rows, and import them.
9. Open a row and confirm Pallet ID, photos, sample size, cut sample, defect counts, and status are on one page.
10. Set a sample size of 5 and confirm primary defect counts cannot total more than 5.
11. Use “Box 2 from same pallet” and confirm the second row is labeled correctly.
12. Mark the cut sample complete, finish the row, and confirm progress updates.
13. Add at least two photos, reorder them, and test Share / Save Image on iPhone.
14. Delete a test row or photo and confirm Undo restores it.
15. Open Report and confirm Final Review flags missing details before completion.
16. Test the app once in airplane mode after it has loaded successfully online.
